# 🏥 HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM — FULL ROLE & FLOW DESIGN

---

# 🧠 SYSTEM OVERVIEW

This system is a **role-based hospital management platform** where:

* Every action follows a **strict workflow**
* Every user has **controlled permissions**
* All operations are enforced via **database constraints + backend logic**

---

# 👥 ROLES IN THE SYSTEM

---

## 🧑 PATIENT (Self-Service User)

### 🎯 Purpose

Interact with the hospital system for personal healthcare needs.

---

### ✅ What Patient CAN do

* View available doctors and their time slots
* Book an appointment with a doctor
* Reschedule or cancel their own appointment
* View their consultations
* View prescribed medicines
* View billing details
* View payment history
* View room/ward (if admitted)

---

### ❌ What Patient CANNOT do

* Modify prescriptions
* Generate bills
* Assign rooms
* Access other patients’ data
* Modify doctor/staff data

---

### 🧠 Key Rule

> Patient can only modify **their own appointments**, nothing else.

---

---

## 👨‍⚕️ DOCTOR (Medical Authority)

### 🎯 Purpose

Handle all medical aspects of patient care.

---

### ✅ What Doctor CAN do

* Set availability slots
* View their own appointments
* View their own consultations
* Prescribe medicines
* Update prescriptions
* Admit patients (assign rooms)
* View bills of their patients
* Cancel their own appointments

---

### ❌ What Doctor CANNOT do

* Access other doctors’ patients
* Modify billing structure
* Manage users

---

### 🧠 Key Rule

> A doctor can only access and modify data **they own (their consultations)**.

---

---

## 🧑‍💼 STAFF (Operational Role)

### 🎯 Purpose

Handle hospital operations and administration tasks.

---

### ✅ What Staff CAN do

* View all patients
* Add/update patient data
* Generate bills
* Record payments
* Manage admissions (rooms/wards)
* Assist in appointment handling

---

### ❌ What Staff CANNOT do

* Modify doctor credentials
* Override doctor ownership (ideally)
* Prescribe medicine

---

### 🧠 Key Rule

> Staff handles **operations, not medical decisions**.

---

---

## 👑 ADMIN (Full Control)

### 🎯 Purpose

System-level control and management.

---

### ✅ What Admin CAN do

* Full access to all data
* Create/delete users (doctor/staff/patient)
* Override all constraints
* Modify any record

---

### 🧠 Key Rule

> Admin has **complete unrestricted control**.

---

# 🔄 SYSTEM WORKFLOW (CORE LOGIC)

---

## 🟢 STEP 1: DOCTOR SETS AVAILABILITY

```text
Doctor → adds available time slots
```

Stored in:

```text
DoctorAvailability
```

---

## 🟢 STEP 2: PATIENT BOOKS APPOINTMENT

```text
Patient → selects doctor + time slot
```

### Backend checks:

* Slot exists
* Slot is NOT booked
* No time conflict

---

### System actions:

```text
1. Create Consultation
2. Create Appointment
3. Mark slot as booked
```

---

## 🟢 STEP 3: CONSULTATION HAPPENS

```text
Doctor → views consultation
```

---

## 🟢 STEP 4: PRESCRIPTION

```text
Doctor → prescribes medicines
```

Stored in:

```text
Prescription
```

---

## 🟢 STEP 5: (OPTIONAL) ADMISSION

```text
Doctor/Staff → assigns room
```

### Constraints:

* One patient = one room
* One room = one patient

---

## 🟢 STEP 6: BILL GENERATION

```text
Staff → generates bill
```

Includes:

* consultation cost
* room cost
* medicines

---

## 🟢 STEP 7: PAYMENT

```text
Patient → pays bill
```

Stored in:

```text
Payment
```

# 🔥 APPOINTMENT SLOT ENFORCEMENT (VERY IMPORTANT)

---

## ✅ YES — YOUR SYSTEM HANDLES THIS PROPERLY

Here’s how:

---

### 1. Unique constraint

```sql
UNIQUE (doctor_id, appointment_datetime)
```

👉 Prevents:

* double booking same doctor

---

### 2. Availability table

```text
DoctorAvailability
```

👉 Defines valid slots

---

### 3. Booking logic

When patient books:

```text
CHECK:
- slot exists
- slot not booked
```

---

### 4. Slot consumption

```text
UPDATE DoctorAvailability
SET is_booked = TRUE
```

👉 Slot cannot be reused

---

### 5. Optional extra protection

```sql
UNIQUE (patient_id, appointment_datetime)
```

👉 prevents patient double booking
